About Us

Amazon is a global technology company founded by Jeff Bezos in 1994. It started as an online bookstore and has since expanded into various sectors, including e-commerce, cloud computing (Amazon Web Services - AWS), digital streaming (Prime Video), and AI-driven devices (like Alexa). Known for its customer-centric approach, Amazon offers fast delivery through Amazon Prime and operates a vast marketplace for third-party sellers. It also owns physical stores like Whole Foods and leads innovations in logistics and smart home technology. Today, Amazon is one of the world's largest companies.

Our Services

Service 1

Amazon Prime: A subscription service providing fast shipping, access to streaming (Prime Video, Prime Music), and exclusive deals for members

Service 2

Amazon Web Services (AWS): A cloud computing platform offering services like storage, databases, and computing power, widely used by businesses and developers.

Service 3

Amazon Marketplace: An online platform where third-party sellers can list and sell their products, with Amazon handling payments and often logistics through fulfillment services.
